Edinburgh Global Online Learning Masters Scholarships
ABOUT

Edinburgh Global Online Learning Masters Scholarships are providing unique opportunities for students to get a Master’s degree from the University of Edinburgh for free. Thanks to the innovation and power of the internet students can get the best education from every part of the world on an online basis while staying in their home countries. The Edinburgh Global Scholarship aims at providing equal opportunities to students of lower-income countries or lower-middle-income countries. The purpose is to create a pool where all the talented and extraordinary minds from all over the globe can connect through education.

Every year 12 scholarships are granted to international students to have online Masters’s classes for free in the UK and enjoy the European education style. For this Masters’s class, the students are not required to attend the classes physically in the university, however, the level of education remains the same. This fully-funded scholarship at the University of Edinburgh is a platform where students can connect to the world’s best faculty and pursue their higher studies in a prestigious institution. The scholarship by the UK-based university is a testimony of the UK’s efforts toward equal education and in removing the border differences and promoting diversity, tolerance, and acceptance.

Edinburgh University comes among the top-ranked universities in the UK. It is renowned for its excellent research culture. Moreover, the university is the top university to receive the maximum number of applications for undergraduates every year. Among its alumni, there are names like Alexander Graham Bell, Charles Darwin, David Hume, and James Clerk Maxwell, and the list continues with Nobel Prize Laureates, Turing Award winners, Pulitzer Prize winners, Abel Prize laureates, and Fields Medalists. Moreover, many heads of state are graduates of the University of Edinburgh. This fully-funded Master’s scholarship is a chance for students from lower-income countries to come to the list of these world-known personalities.

Requirments

📌The candidate must be commencing the online part-time learning Masters’s program offered by the University in session 2023-2024

📌The candidates must be residents of the eligible countries.

📌Must-Have at least two years of work experience

📌The candidate must have secured a seat for an online Masters at the University of Edinburgh.

Benefits

The full tuition fee will be covered by this scholarship.

The student does not have to travel to the UK for the course and can continue to stay in the home country.

The students will get an education from the best faculty in the world
