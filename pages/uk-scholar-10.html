Queen Mary University Deep mind Scholarship
ABOUT

Queen Mary University DeepMind Scholarship 2023 in the UK is an excellent opportunity for UK or international students belonging to any nationality. The underrepresented groups are encouraged to apply here. This fully-funded scholarship is designed to support and encourage those groups to continue their postgraduate studies in the international environment. The courses available for the master’s level involve the following fields: Artificial Intelligence, Computer Sciences, and Machine Learning. These scholarships promote black students and female candidates as they are being suppressed in the mentioned areas of study. Therefore, the eligible candidates should not miss the chance to avail this opportunity as the lucky ones will make their slots here.

The Queen Mary University of London scholarship will provide significant investment in your future career. The generous packages of scholarships support you all in every way, whether in personal development or professional growth. The university aims to create an inclusive environment for everyone by empowering the suppressed strata of our society. It is envisioned to build a cherished cultural diversity by offering distinct study areas. The students and staff feel fully nourished by engaging themselves in different educational activities and feel proud to be a part of the world’s best university in the United Kingdom. Through these UK scholarships, the university aims to create a better world by generating new avenues of knowledge, challenging existing knowledge, and engaging everyone at local, national, and international levels.

The fully-funded international scholarships for international students in Europe revolve around maintaining the proud tradition of nurturing and persuading talented students and staff irrespective of their political, economic, cultural, social, and other backgrounds. The scholarship aims to increase the vital connections among local and global communities through constructive dialogues. The scholarship program is based on eliminating differences by working collectively. People associated with the university are ambitious, innovative, and creative in their thoughts. They respond to new developmental paths with their optimistic imaginations and likewise further the University’s mission, vision, and academic objectives. Such a platform is formulated to encourage a collegial community through openness, understanding, cooperation, listening, and co-creation of the collegial approach.

The Queen Mary University of London DeepMind scholarship is offered by maintaining the highest ethical standards and integrity in every organization’s activity. Throughout history, the university has fostered an environment of social justice and uplifted the lives of communities by inculcating values of academic excellence. Through challenging activities, people are forced to think out of the box, and when their opinions collide with each other, disciplines interact, and perspectives intersect, refined and accurate thinking originates. Moreover, people will have a great experience working with all ethnicities and developing viable solutions to each prevalent global problem. This is a great opportunity to visit a land full of beautiful landscapes, flowing mountains, and refreshing beaches.

Deadline

The application deadline for the scholarship is 5 June 2023.

Apply now
Requirments

📌Everyone from any nationality can apply to either the UK or an international student.

📌Should be female or belong to any underrepresented group, especially black students.

📌Belongs to one of the following ethnicities: Black or/and White African, Black or/and White Caribbean, or other mixed backgrounds.

📌Confirmed offer to study in any of the available courses.

📌 Complete the application form.

📌CV

📌Offer letter

📌Personal statement of a maximum of 500 words

Benefits

The scholarship will cover the cost of tuition fees, i.e. £12,250.

For international students, the cost of the tuition fee is £28,950.

Living allowance of £15,480.

Annual travel scholarship of £2,200.
