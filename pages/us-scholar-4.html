Knight Hennessy Scholarship Program (Stanford University)
ABOUT

Knight Hennessy Scholarship Program 2024 is for national and international students who wish to pursue a graduate degree from Stanford University. The Hennessy scholarship is a unique opportunity for graduate students from across Stanford University to get involved in multicultural and diverse activities. Moreover, the fully-funded scholarship in the USA for international graduates as well as for national graduates is a platform that delivers a range of educational experiences which enable the graduates to address the complex challenges of the contemporary world. Furthermore, Stanford University warmly welcomes undocumented students of the USA to obtain their higher education on campus. The Knight-Hennessy Scholars Program 2024 is an outstanding opportunity for those graduates who have visionary minds and are eager to learn the cross-cultural dimensions of the world. Moreover, the students who exhibit leadership qualities from their prior experience and who are role models for others in bringing positive changes are highly likely to this top scholarship in the USA. Stanford University Scholarship is for every graduate regardless of nationality, color, ethnicity, age, and gender. The purpose of this Stanford scholarship is to promote the culture of collaboration and community services. In addition, the scholarship at Stanford is for graduates of every discipline and is not limited to only JD, MA, MBAMD, MFA, MS, DMA, and PhD programs.

Under this Knight-Hennessy Scholarship Program, the selected students will get a chance to become an integral part of the King Global Leadership Program (KGLP) as well. In pursuing their studies, will be getting an in-depth insight into leadership and technical skills. In addition, the scholars will be equipped enough to solve difficult problems through their creativity and will learn a unique decision-making technique under pressure and uncertain conditions. Furthermore, Stanford University has a vice-provost for Graduate Education which offers all graduate students a comprehensive professional development framework. In order to provide guidance to the students, the community of the Knight-Hennessy Scholars and the coaches always remain available to other scholars. It also builds harmony and community bonding among them.

The Knight-Hennessy Scholars Program is a platform for development and learning. At Stanford University, there is a Dening House which is home to almost 9000 national and international students. The community of scholars gets a chance to polish their interpersonal and collaborative skills by interacting with others. Stanford University Dening House also serves as a hub where Knight-Hennessy Scholars share their ideas and experiences with other scholars while staying close to each other. Besides, this Dening House was awarded the Design Award 2020 BY AI A Silicon Valley. It means, that the environment, calmness, and beauty of the house also affect the scholars in a very positive and productive manner who spend their time in Denning House. The scholars get a chance to conduct small gatherings, conferences, events, regular dinners, etc in this house which is the basis of their mutual understanding and cultural exchange. The following article further elaborates on other prominent features of this best scholarship in the USA which is one other than the Knight-Hennessy Scholarship 2024 for graduates.

Deadline

The application deadline for the scholarship is October 11, 2023, while The application deadline for Stanford varies depending on the program. It is recommended to check your desired program to verify the deadline for KHS applicants. It’s important to note that KHS applicants must submit their application for the graduate program by December 1, 2023, or by the program’s standard deadline if it is earlier. Apply now

Requirments



📌 Graduates from every nationality, ethnicity, gender, or age can apply for the scholarship.

📌 In order to be eligible for the scholarship, the applicant must be enrolled and accepted in a full-time Stanford graduate degree program.

📌The applicant must be a newly enrolled student in a graduate program.

📌The applicant must have earned a bachelor’s degree in January 2017 or later.

📌In the case of applicants who are from the US military (active or veteran), their degree must be earned in January 2015 or later.

📌The applicant must fulfill these three criteria: 1) Independence of thought 2) Purposeful leadership 3) Civic Mindset.

📌 TOEFL is accepted by all the Stanford graduate programs. However, the MBA program also accepts IELTS and PTE.

📌For more information about the Knight-Hennessy Scholarship requirements visit the official website.

Benefits

The scholars will receive a funding grant for up to three years from the time of admission and selection.

A living stipend will be paid.

A living and education stipend will be paid which will cover room and board, books, academic supplies, instructional material, local transport costs, and reasonable personal expenses.

A stipend to cover tuition and associated fees

An economy class return ticket to and from Stanford will be given annually.

A one-time relocation stipend

A supplementary grant will be provided to enrich educational insight through conferences and travel.

If the scholars choose summer research projects as additional learning, they will be given a special summer grant for the course.
