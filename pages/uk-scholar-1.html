Oxford Pershing Square Graduate Scholarships 2024-2025 | Fully Funded | Study in UK
ABOUT

The Oxford Pershing Square Graduate Scholarships 2024-2025 in the UK is one of the prestigious scholarships for international students who want to pursue their MBA and master’s in the United Kingdom. Through this Oxford Pershing Square scholarship, international students can pursue master’s programs at Oxford University for the academic year 2024-25. The Oxford Pershing Square Graduate Scholarships are fully funded scholarships for international students at the University of Oxford, which means that they can study for free in the UK with this Oxford Scholarship. The fully funded scholarship in the UK will also provide an opportunity for all excellent students to study in Oxford for free. The Pershing scholarship in the UK is accepting students from all over the world. The Oxford University scholarship is a great chance for talented students who cannot study at any international university due to their unaffordability to study for free in the UK.

The University of Oxford will award up to 6 fully funded scholarships for international students to study at such an amazing university in the UK and get a chance to be a part of Oxford. Students from all over the world with any nationality can apply for the fully funded Oxford University Pershing scholarship. As the Oxford Pershing Square Graduate Scholarships at the University of Oxford are fully funded, the application process is free of charge. This fully funded UK scholarship covers the full tuition fee and also provides £15,609 as living expenses for both years of study. Every year, full scholarships are awarded by the Pershing Square Foundation to support excellent 1+ 1 MBA students, which covers both the masters and the MBA year. Fully funded UK scholarships are an excellent chance to get into the world’s top and most renowned university every brilliant student dreams of studying at.

Pershing Square Scholars are those outstanding students who have the ability and dedication to discover sustainable and scalable alternatives to culture-related problems on a global scale. The Oxford Pershing Square Graduate scholarships allow students to pursue any of the associated master’s degrees and merge them with an MBA. The fully-funded Oxford scholarship is available to those students who are currently enrolled in any of the master’s partnering degree and then wants to continue their MBA. The Oxford Pershing Graduate Scholarship was created in 2014, and it offers study-related expenses, university fees, and an extra amount provided to students so that they can support their living costs for both years of study in the UK.

Through this graduate scholarship in the UK, international students will get an opportunity to get an education and mentoring from experts at the top university in the UK. They will also get an amazing chance to international networking with different inspiring people and organizations that are related to the Pershing Square Foundation community. Pershing Square Scholars also get chances to participate in different renowned meetings, conferences, and activities. Therefore do not miss this opportunity to pursue a master’s program/MBA in the UK and apply for the Oxford Pershing Square Graduate Scholarships today! However, before proceeding to the application, check the details below to ensure your eligibility.

Deadline

You must apply by 5 January 2024 for the 1+1 MBA and by your chosen Master's programme deadline, either in December or January. Funding deadlines for 1+1 MBA partnering programmes can be found on the relevant course page on the Graduate Admissions website.

Requirments

📌Applicants must have a good command of English.

📌International students from all over the world are eligible for the Oxford Pershing Square Scholarship 2024-2025 in the UK.

📌Applicants must have excellent academic achievements.

📌Applicants must have leadership potential, which can be shown through their experience and motivation.

📌Furthermore, they must have strong personal character, commitment, and integrity.

📌Applicants must have the intention to work on a global scale of social and cultural problems.

📌The applicants must have the ability to create ideas on how we can achieve scalable and sustainable solutions to these global challenges.

📌Applicants must know properly and have a vision of how the Oxford 1+1 MBA will help them to fulfil their objectives.

Benefits

Oxford Pershing Square Scholarship will provide full tuition fees for the scholars.

The scholarship will also provide £17,668 to scholars, which will be used to cover the living expenses of students for both years of study.

A chance to study for free in the UK. The scholarship offers international networking opportunities.

Moreover, Oxford Pershing Square Scholarship in UK is an amazing opportunity to be a part of the University of Oxford and study for free at Oxford.

A chance to attend different international conferences and activities.
