Chulabhorn Graduate Institute Scholarship[Thailand]
ABOUT

Chulabhorn Graduate Institute Scholarship in Thailand 2024 welcomes those individuals who wish to go abroad for studies but whose financial circumstances do not allow them to pursue their dreams. This CGI Scholarship 2024 attracts all students belonging to different nationalities, origins, races, and religions to reach their career destinations through excellent international academic studies. You can apply for this fabulous option if you have a CGPA of 2.75 in your Bachelor’s degree in applied biological, environmental, and chemical sciences and have excellent proficiency in the English language. This will prove to be a transformative career ladder in your professional career through nourishing your personality in an internationally refined environment.

The fully-funded scholarship to study in Thailand aims to help you overcome your financial burden by granting you tuition fees, multiple academic and accommodation allowances, and a handsome amount in the form of a monthly stipend. This Thailand scholarship 2023 is based on gender equality and free from discrimination; people of all genders and socioeconomic backgrounds are eligible for this Chulabhorn graduate institute scholarship. The courses under the Master’s program, offered by the renowned institute of Thailand, will help you learn the dynamics of your related field of science in a more comprehensive way. The program may involve various academic activities and project-focused assignments that will harness the actual hidden potential of your learned skills during your tenure with Chulabhorn Royal Academy.

The scholarship for international students is offered by the well-recognized multi-disciplinary post-graduate institute. The institute was established in 2005 through an initiative of Dr. Her Royal Highness Princess Chulabhorn Mahidol. The institute intends to produce effective leaders and thinkers through the advanced teaching techniques that are being used in the leading educational and research institutes around the globe. Thus, making the students able to serve their countries in a better way to achieve levels of sustainable development. Please note that there are only 15 scholarships, which will be available for international students on a merit basis. The fully-funded international scholarship opportunity will rate you through the statement of purpose, your English language scores, your experience in scientific and laboratory research, and your academic scores. Be sure that you are at the top in each category to acquire the position through the CGI, Chulabhorn Graduate Institute Post-graduate Scholarship. Moreover, you will have the privilege to visit a country full of tropical beaches, ancient temples, and royal palaces. It has a chaotic charm, which attracts a number of visitors to come and enjoy the beauties of the place. Most interestingly, you will feel refreshed and never disappointed throughout your tenure there because the country is globally recognized as the Land of Smiles.
