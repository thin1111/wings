University of Debrecen Stipendium Hungaricum Scholarship [Hungary]
ABOUT

If you are searching for a fully funded scholarship for a bachelor’s, master’s, or doctoral level then you should definitely avail this opportunity to study in Hungary. International students can now apply for the University of Debrecen Stipendium Hungaricum Scholarship 2023. Europe is a dream place for international students and it is a dream of every student to pursue their higher education at world-class universities in Europe. If you want to study abroad but are worried about the high fees and other expenses, don’t worry, because we are bringing you a fully funded international scholarship that is going to turn your dreams into reality.

Hungary aims to attract a large number of dedicated and passionate brilliant young minds from around the world so they may get the golden chance to receive a quality education and pursue their dreams in their fields of interest. The Hungarian government is providing all the funds to candidates to get a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to study in the heart of Europe. A company of like-minded people who are striving for change is going to be a valuable asset. Students will interact with people from other regions and learn about their cultures and traditions.

This scholarship for international students in Hungary at the University of Debrecen allows students to complete bachelor’s, master’s, or doctoral degrees in a wide range of disciplines. If you are a researcher and looking for exposure to world-class research, modern equipped laboratories, and a variety of facilities, this is the opportunity for you. One of the principal advantages of this Europe scholarship is that it covers all expenditures, from tuition to medical expenses.

The Hungarian government initiated this program in 2013 to attract bright students from all over the world. Until now, the Hungarian Government has collaborated with 68 sending partners, allowing a large number of dynamic candidates to experience the Hungarian educational system at Hungary’s top-rated institutes. Students are evaluated based on their excellent academic records and their motivation to give back to their community to bring positive change.

Requirments

Everyone from the eligible country can apply.

📌Your study program must be related to your country’s areas of development.

📌Highly intellectual students will be preferred.

📌Committed to leaving Australia after the completion of the scholarship.

📌Strategic thinking.

📌Results achievement.

📌Productive working relationships.

📌Communication skills.

📌Negotiation skills.

📌 Personal integrity.

Benefits

Your full tuition fee will be covered by this scholarship.

Students will be given a monthly stipend.

Bachelor’s and master’s students will get a stipend of 120 euros every month till the completion of their studies.

Doctoral students will get a monthly stipend of 331 euros till the completion of their degree.

All your expenses related to accommodation will be covered by this scholarship.

Students will be provided free medical insurance.
