Asian Development Bank Scholarship[Singapore]
ABOUT

Call for application for the Asian Development Bank Scholarship 2023-24. Asian Development Bank has announced marvelous career opportunities for students holding a Bachelor’s degree and unable to pursue their education due to financial and other circumstances. ADB promotes you to participate in the fields of economics, management sciences, technology, law and public policy, and other developmental fields. This ADB scholarship 2023 aims to uplift developing countries by investing in their human capital and enabling them to contribute to their home countries. Through this opportunity, ADB is willing to maintain diplomatic ties with its member countries. Moreover, the fully-funded scholarship program is organized to ensure cultural diversity within the institute by allowing people to join in. The fully-funded scholarships in Asian Development member countries attract people intending to share knowledge, skills, and abilities. The master’s degree scholarship helps enable people to become a part of its vision in achieving the core values of sustainability, prosperity, inclusiveness, resilience, and development in the Asia and Pacific regions. ADB intends to ensure regional integration and create an inclusive environment where all forms of diversity are recognized, valued, and respected. No matter where you belong, what is your gender, nationality, ethnicity, religion, what is your sexual orientation, whether you are physically disabled, or what is your thinking style?

The Asia and Pacific scholarship opportunity is devoted to transforming your lives with honor. ADB considers everyone a unique entity because it believes that all of you, with unique abilities, can mark valuable contributions to making the region a better place. This fully-funded international scholarship program encourages women to apply for this fully-funded opportunity and feel empowered. The academic activities and the assignments will help you develop social networking with your peers. Moreover, under the guidance of supervisors, having diversified knowledge will help you counter field-related challenges in solution-driven ways. Thus, it will lead you to become an influential market professional.

The Asian Development Bank offers full scholarships to alleviate extreme poverty by ensuring economic growth in the region. ADB also assists its member countries and partners by providing the required loans, technical assistance, and special grants. Moreover, ADB also guarantees equity-based investments in the regions to stimulate social, political, and economic developments there. ADB is an open community comprising government officials, researchers, students, and civil society organizations eager to participate in national development efforts. Further, this opportunity will help you interact with area experts, policymakers, and other specialized practitioners of the related fields.

Deadline

The application deadline for the scholarship has not been mentioned yet, so you can apply from now onwards.

Requirments



📌Having the nationality of an ADB borrowing country.

📌Having the nationality of Japanese ODA.

📌Do not have dual citizenship in any developed country.

📌The applicant must be proficient in the English language.

📌Be in good health.

📌Secured master’s admission at a designated institution.

📌Possess bachelor’s degree with excellent academic scores.

📌Minimum 2 years of full-time working experience after a university degree.

📌Proficient in English (written and verbal) language.

📌Not more than 35 years of age (in certain cases, it is extendable if the Government of Japan endorses).

📌Committed to returning home after completion of studies for at least two years.

📌Not be associated with working with ADB.

📌Not be the close relative of any employee of ADB.

📌Not be working or living in the country except his or her home country.

📌Not enrolled in graduate degree programs.

📌Latest income tax return/certification of annual or monthly salary.

📌Certification of family income

📌Copies of academic records.

📌Complete the application form.

Benefits

Full tuition fees.

Traveling allowances.

Monthly subsistence allowance.

Housing allowance.

Books and other study material allowance.

The settling-in allowance will be provided Medical insurance.

Special research grant for research scholars.

Computer literacy, preparatory language, and other similar courses can also be covered.
