Lester B.Pearson Scholarship
ABOUT

Lester B. Pearson Scholarship in Canada 2024 is an opportunity for bright minds to study at one of the best universities in the world – the University of Toronto. The University of Toronto is the world’s leading institute in research and teaching. The fully-funded scholarship at Toronto University is thus a chance for undergraduates to pursue their academic careers in closely-knit learning communities. In addition, the Lester B. Pearson International Scholarship is an opportunity for international students to spend four consecutive years of their lives in a multicultural and diverse city. The change will help them to groom themselves as more resilient and tolerant persons. The fully-funded undergraduate scholarships in Canada are awarded annually to the undergraduates who are being nominated by their high schools on the basis of their extraordinary and outstanding educational performance. Each year 37 students are selected as Lester B. scholars who continue their undergraduate studies at the University of Toronto. Besides the academic achievements of the students, they are also assessed by their life behavior and their future expected impact on the global community. It is expected from the Lester B. Pearson Scholars to have a positive change in this world and contribute towards a better future through their creativity.

The University of Toronto Scholarships recipients will get a lot of financial benefits along with the chance of grooming in the modern world. The international Pearson scholars can be from any field being taught at the University of Toronto ranging from humanities, social sciences, life sciences, physics, mathematical sciences, sciences, commerce and management, engineering, kinesiology, physical education, and music to architecture. Thus the students who are brilliant must try for this fully-funded international scholarship in Canada. The details about the eligibility and application process are discussed in the following lines.

The young students must apply for the Lester scholarship for international students in Canada because of its affiliation with the world’s best university as well as the surety of the students for four years in the world’s safest city. As in the world ranking the city of Toronto is rated as the safest and best place to live. Moreover, the University of Toronto’s ranking in the world is 18 according to the Times Higher Education – WUR 2023. The city also attracts world best class minds in business, culture, and education as it hosts more than 30 annual festivals. The global participate in these festivals and promotes cultural harmony and diversity. So, if you fulfill the given requirement, you must not have the chance to apply for this fully-funded Lester B. International Scholarship for undergraduates in Canada.

Deadline

The deadline for the nominating student is November 30, 2023.

The deadline for the OUAC (Ontario Universities Application Centre) application is December 15, 2023.

Pearson Scholarship Deadline: January 15, 2024. Apply now

Requirments



📌All international students can apply.

📌The applicant must not be a Canadian national.

📌An international student who has a high school in Canada can also apply.

📌The applicant must be nominated by the school or college.

📌The applicant must be a current student in the final year of secondary school.

📌In case the applicant is graduated from high school, the graduation should not be earlier than June 2023.

📌 The applicants must have applied to study at the University of Toronto.

📌The applicants must not have started their post-secondary studies at any institute.

📌The applicant must be a first-entry graduate.

Benefits

Full tuition fees for four years will be covered.

Book allowance will be given.

Incidental costs will be fully covered.

Accommodation is fully covered by the host organization.
