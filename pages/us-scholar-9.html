Harvard University MBA Scholarship
ABOUT

Harvard University MBA Scholarship Program 2025 is one of the best business scholarship programs when it comes to the level of learning and opportunities. Brilliant students from all over the world can have a bright chance to study at the worldâ€™s best university without paying any tuition fee due to this fully funded Harvard scholarship. Without this top scholarship, most commendable students could never think of going to Harvard for higher studies due to its costly courses; but this scholarship has made this dream come true. Harvard University MBA scholarship is also known as the Boustany MBA Harvard Scholarship. This is because of the fact that the scholarship is being granted by the Boustany Foundation. The purpose of granting the fully-funded scholarship at Harvard for international students is the betterment of mankind. The Boustany Foundation is eager to provide opportunities for better education to the students from less privileged classes. Moreover, the program also serves as a cultural exchange program because it is in the mandate of the foundation that the world must be a place where people can accept other cultures as well.

The most prominent and distinguishing feature of this fully-funded international scholarship at Harvard University is the internship at the Boustany Foundation. During their course study, the students are required to do a two-month internship at the foundation. The students in this way get a sure chance to work on the projects of the Boustany Foundation and learn the practical realities of the world. This experience enhances their knowledge and skill in implementing the theory into practice. Moreover, the projects are being supervised by the think tanks and the best minds in the world. Apart from this, the students get the chance to travel to Monaco for two months of internship for free.

The fully-funded MBA scholarship is no doubt for those students who are not only excellent in their academics but they want to change this world into a better place to live in. it is the aim of the foundation to help those people who have the potential to bring positive impacts in the world full of chaos. The unique thing about this Harvard Scholarship is that this is not an annual scholarship but it is granted to MBA students at Harvard every two years. The MBA class is for two years and after the completion of the former batch, the foundation grants the Harvard scholarships to the students of the next MBA batch.
