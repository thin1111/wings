UAL International Postgraduate Scholarships in the UK
ABOUT

UAL International Postgraduate Scholarships in the UK are available for students desiring to get financial support to pursue their studies after graduation while moving away from home and staying in the UK. This University of Arts London scholarship caters to those students who belong to low-income countries and whose annual household income is euro 50,000 or less than that. No matter from which continent you belong, the UK Government is here to welcome you with open arms. UAL is recognized as the most extensive factory globally, comprised of 6 colleges with a total strength of 19000+ students from everywhere. Moreover, it is also considered the second-top university in the world in Art and Design. You are welcome to come and join the network that creates the innovators for tomorrow.

The UK scholarships at UAL aim to build your dream of studying in an internationally recognized institute. These international scholarships are free from any constraints and are designed to overcome the prevalent challenges of the globe in a feasible manner. You can learn from expert tutors in the area of your interest. Further, you can benefit from modern facilities, extensive business links, and career assistance. You will have the privilege to be inspired by the UK’s culture, academics, and residence as it is symbolized as the world’s creative capital. UAL invites you to the UK with the assurance to facilitate you with an environment full of productivity and creativity.

The scholarship to study abroad in one of the best universities in the UK will be a valuable addition to your academic and professional profile. Thus, changing the dimensions of your personality in all aspects. UAL is standing upon the outstanding achievements of its students and staff. The collaborative staff at UAL has cultivated a diverse learning and development culture, which inspires students to come with high hopes and translate their knowledge into practicality. UAL’s distinctive teaching style helps students become successful professionals, designers, and artists. Moreover, the award-winning careers are tailor-made as they cater to each individual as per their anticipated need.

These UAL Scholarships encompass multiple benefits, including the most expensive accommodation cost and tuition fees of the London institutes, which is otherwise very difficult for ordinary people to bear such expenses. This world-class university has been driving the projections to shape global industries creatively. UAL’s teaching faculty consists of professional artists, designers, technicians, practitioners, theorists, and others ready to deliver cutting-edge programs to students. UAL is home to a number of diverse students from different countries, and it ensures to offer a supportive and inclusive environment to everyone. Concerning the career program, its dedicated focus is on offering high-quality engagements with industrial partners so that the students will be able to win opportunities in the future.

Deadline

The application deadline for this scholarship is Friday 30 June 2023.

Apply now
Requirments

📌You must be an ordinarily resident of any of the low-income countries or an asylum seeker in the UK.

📌You must not possess postgraduate qualifications before.

📌Your annual household income should be Euro 50,000 or below than that.

📌You must be able to answer the following questions with maximum 350 words of each:

o How this postgraduate scholarship will make a difference in your career?.

o How this qualification will support your future plans?

o How you will address the challenges of your home country?

o How you will endorse the values of social justice and environmental stewardship?

📌Copy of immigration documents.

📌Copy of ID card.

📌Formal proof of address.

📌Invitation letter from the University.

Benefits

The scholarships cover tuition fees.

The scholarships will provide accommodation at UAL’s hall of residence.

The scholarship will help enrich the academic knowledge of the particular area of interest.

It will help induce market-competitive skills among the participants.
