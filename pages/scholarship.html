Preparation Tips For Scholarship

Do you wanna go to school abroad. Parents can't afford it. And don't know how to fix it.... We will share you what you need to prepare to apply for scholarships and the countries you can apply for.

1) Strong Academic Record

If you want to get a good scholarship, the number one most important thing is to have -good grades- in your high school education.

2) Research Scholarship Early

What do you need? , What to prepare?, how does it given ? , What is the last day? , How long Is it take? From government ? From private school? From foundation? At least you'll have to take one or two yeasr to understand.

3) Strong Resume

It's something most young people don't notice. If you are focused on getting a scholar and plan to go to school abroad, Volunteer, Internship, Work, etc must be doing. We would like you to be aware that these are very powerful activities for your resume.

4) Good Relationships with Professors or Mentors

A recommendation letter is very important for schools with a good ranking, whether it's a scholarship or going to school on your own budget. The school professors , If you're working, it's better to get to know your mentors and bosses.

5) Language Proficiency

You must prepare to get a good score (at least 7.0) such as IELTS, which is a certification of language skills

6) Apply Multiple Scholarships

When applying for scholarships, don't focus on just one. If you don't get one, reserve one and apply for more schools that suit you.

After preparing these things, if some schools often call for interviews, you should study and prepare for the interviews step. Some say that scholarship is depending on luck. Luck means work. If you are persistent and don't want to be too early, if you can devote time and effort to prepare it, one day, one year, you can definitely do it.
