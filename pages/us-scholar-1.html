EWC Graduate Degree Fellowship
ABOUT

East West Centre Graduate Degree Fellowship in the United States welcomes committed, hardworking, and passionate young graduates from around the globe. Everyone, having a Bachelor’s degree from a higher institution abroad or from U.S. based institute can apply for this remarkable opportunity in the United States. This scholarship in the USA will help the fellows to work with East West Centre to develop themselves in the following areas: educational, cultural, and residential community building, along with leadership development. Moreover, the participants will be engaged in their post-graduation studies at the University of Hawai’i simultaneously. This international fellowship opportunity in the United States is not only an opportunity of receiving financial aid but the primary purpose of this scholarship is to inculcate the sense of Asia Pacific community among the individuals and prepare them for becoming future leaders. This is an incredible opportunity for individuals to reside together and interact with each other while engaging in intellectual and social activities. EWC fellowship will help you learn about team-building activities, the Asia Pacific region, leadership development projects and internships, educational enrichment gatherings, cultural exchange, community service, and service-learning projects. Moreover, it will expand your social network by establishing friendship ties to positively change the Asia Pacific region.

The fully funded scholarships for international students in USA will cover the following expenses of the University of Hawai’i graduates: books, housing in an EWC dormitory, health insurance, partial funding towards meals, and incidental expenses. Moreover, based on competitiveness, additional funding for field study, attending conferences, and conducting presentations are also offered. The EWC is considered unique among international institutions due to its active focus on team building at the campus level, which ultimately extends to community building at the Asia Pacific regional level. While learning and working together, individuals extend helping hands to the local community to bring developments around the surroundings.

Talking about the EWC Graduate Degree Fellowship 2024, the University of Hawai’i is regarded as the flagship campus of the state. Since its foundation in 1970, it had been expanded over 320 acres of land in Manoa Valley. It is a research-based university, which is offering bachelor’s degrees and master’s degrees, and doctoral degrees in multiple fields. EWC sponsors fellowships in those fields which are associated with its driving mission. We are living in an era of increased economic, social, and technological interdependence world, in which leaders must possess knowledge based on multicultural perspectives, regional literacy, and a shared sense of community. Such steps by institutes help not only individuals to excel in their careers but also the nations to grow as per their national and global objectives.
