About-Us

AIM

To establish a location where at least one person can feel comfortable and find knowledge by sharing link trees of resources for teachers and students, providing more information about scholarship options, spreading awareness for students who plan to study abroad, and providing more information about these chances.

VISION

We see that resources and educational information are needed for students and teachers in different ways. We hope to get at least one person to get comfortable and easy to find resources in one place. If there are more and more educational and informative websites, students and teachers are more likely to move on.

MISSION

Our mission is to tell everyone about resources for learning, as well as scholarship program collections. In particular for teachers and students on their educational journeys, we want you to be able to find all of our collections in one location.
