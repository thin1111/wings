Gates Cambridge Scholarship 2024-25 is an outstanding opportunity for those students who have excellent intellectual abilities and are eager to improve the lives of others as well. Gates Scholarship is one of the leading universities in the world that aims at creating an interactive community of future world leaders who can bring a better and more optimistic change in the world. The Gate scholars at Cambridge University on a diverse range of topics like innovations, challenges, and solutions. Thus through the Gates Cambridge Trust scholarship in the UK, the students also contribute to the university’s own mission which is to contribute to the welfare of society.

The Gates Scholarship is the largest donation to any single UK university. As the Gates Foundation is always persistent in fighting poverty, disease, and inequality around the world, it has donated US $120m for this full-cost scholarship. This is proof of the organization’s seriousness about promoting equality. The fully-funded international scholarship in the UK for international students is a platform for extraordinary and skillful students from where they can stop into the world’s most prestigious university without worrying about their financial conditions. In order to apply correctly for this Cambridge scholarship read the following instructions carefully.

Deadline

The deadline for International students is either 5 December 2023 or 4 January 2024, depending on the course.
The deadline for US citizens is 11 October 2023.

Apply now
Requirments

📌Personal statement of no more than 3000 characters.

📌The PhD candidates have to submit a research proposal. Even if the application for a course or college does not require a research proposal, the Gates scholarship has this requirement as a mandatory one.

📌Three reference letters (One can be from a current employee if needed.

Benefits

The total study cost will be covered by this scholarship.

The visa cost will be covered.

An economy class one-time airfare will be provided.

The cost of immigration Health Surcharge will be covered.

Additional academic development funds up to £500-£2000 will be given to attend conferences and other courses. However, the amount depends on the course length.

The scholarship covers a family allowance of up to £11,604 for one child and up to £16,548 for two or more children.

The scholarship also covers hardship costs or the cost of any unforeseen difficulty.

Fieldwork Allowance

Maternity/Paternity funding
