Marconi International University Scholarships
ABOUT

At Marconi International University, you can apply for plenty of scholarships that means more chances of selection. Marconi International University Scholarships 2021 in the USA welcome students from every corner of the world regardless of any discrimination on the basis of color, caste. Religion, language, etc. Scholarships providing by the Marconi International University are based on categories and different requirements. Moreover, these scholarships are being offered to domestic as well as international students.

Marconi International University aims to provide affordable and accessible education to the students. The scholarships are highly competitive and available in diverse fields of study. Students from different regions join the program and make a culturally rich on-campus experience.

Marconi International Scholarships are offered at bachelor’s, master’s, and postgraduate degree levels. All students will be able to study on scholarship in the USA, will be making everlasting memories and professional communications that will help them in the future.

Deadline

The application deadline of the MIU Scholarships varies according to the scholarship category.

Requirments



📌OAS (The Organization of American States) Scholarships
• Candidates from 35 independent states of the USA can apply for this scholarship
• A candidate can apply to an undergraduate or graduate degree program

📌The students must be excellent in academics.
• Candidates from Latin America and the Caribbean can apply for this scholarship.
• A candidate must submit all academic transcripts.
• A candidate must have completed high school or bachelor’s degree from a recognized institution in order to apply to the bachelor’s or master’s degree program.
• A candidate should be willing to take the course online.

📌OAS (The Organization of American States) Scholarships
• Candidates from Africa, Asia, or the Middle East can apply for this scholarship.
• A candidate must submit all academic transcripts.
• A candidate must have completed high school or bachelor’s degree from a recognized institution in order to apply to the bachelor’s or master’s degree program.


📌African, Asian, and Middle East Undergraduate and Graduate Scholarship
• Candidates from Africa, Asia, or the Middle East can apply for this scholarship.
• A candidate must submit all academic transcripts.
• A candidate must have completed high school or bachelor’s degree from a recognized institution in order to apply to the bachelor’s or master’s degree program.
• A candidate should be willing to take the course online.

📌European Undergraduate and Graduate Scholarship
• Candidates from Europe can apply for this scholarship.
• A candidate must submit all academic transcripts.
• A candidate must have completed high school or bachelor’s degree from a recognized institution in order to apply to the bachelor’s or master’s degree program.
• A candidate should be willing to take the course online.

📌Military and Veterans Scholarship
• A candidate should be willing to take the course online.
• A candidate can either take the course online or on-campus.

📌Florida Resident Adult and Professional Students
• A candidate should be a resident of one of the states, Florida.
• Age range between 28 to 55 years.
• A candidate can either take the course online or on-campus.
• A candidate must have at least 2 years of professional experience.
• A candidate can apply for a master’s or doctoral degree program.

📌Merit-Based Undergraduate and Graduate Scholarship
• A candidate can apply for a master’s or doctoral degree program.
• A candidate can apply at the bachelor’s, master’s, or doctoral degree level.
• A candidate must have at least a 2.9 GPA to apply.

📌Excellence Scholarships
• International students in the USA are eligible to apply.
• A candidate has to write a motivational letter comprising 300 words.
• A candidate has to submit the CV along with the application form.
